Theory Test Simulation

The developer was inspired to create this site after taking the real theory exam and failing twice.

The style, timing and difficulty of the full simulation match those of the real exam. Answers to incorrect questions are revealed only upon finishing the test. Navigating through questions using the browser's "back" button may lead to error messages. Question wording matches that of the real exam (i.e. occasionally confusing and with often non-native sounding English).

All questions are randomly selected from a database of 150 test questions adapted from the Driving License Requisition Manual issued by the Thailand Department of Land Transport.
